About the Appointment System

This web application is designed to help students and teachers easily schedule and manage appointments for various consultations and meetings.

Features include:

  • Booking, canceling, and rescheduling appointments.
  • User-friendly dashboards for both students and teachers.
  • Simple and efficient appointment management system.

Built with Flask and SQLAlchemy for a seamless user experience.
